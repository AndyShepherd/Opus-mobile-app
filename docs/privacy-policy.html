Privacy Policy

Opus Accountancy Practice Manager (Mobile) — Last updated: 28 February 2026

Overview

Opus Accountancy Practice Manager ("Opus", "the app") is an internal business tool used by authorised staff to manage client records. The app is not intended for public use.

Data We Collect

The app itself does not collect, track, or share any personal data with third parties. All data accessed through the app is stored on your organisation's own server.

  • Login credentials — Your username and password are sent directly to your organisation's server to authenticate your session. They are not sent anywhere else.
  • Biometric data — If you enable Face ID or Touch ID, your credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain with biometric protection. Biometric data itself never leaves your device and is managed entirely by iOS.
  • Client data — Client records (names, contact details, company information) are fetched from and stored on your organisation's server. The app does not cache or transmit this data to any external service.

Data Storage

  • Authentication tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain, encrypted by the operating system.
  • Biometric-protected credentials use a separate Keychain store with device-only access control.
  • No data is stored in iCloud, analytics services, or any third-party cloud.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. It communicates solely with your organisation's Opus server.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose any data to third parties.

Your Rights

Since all data resides on your organisation's server, contact your system administrator to access, correct, or delete your data. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored credentials from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact your organisation's Opus system administrator.
